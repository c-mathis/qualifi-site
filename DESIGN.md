## Brand & Style

The design system is anchored in "Clean Square Luxury"—a visual strategy that blends the authoritative weight of premium editorial design with the high-octane energy of modern growth marketing. It is built for a professional audience that values strategic precision and measurable results.

The style is **Minimalist High-Contrast**. By utilizing a dominant deep black canvas, the design system creates a sense of "blackspace" that allows data and growth metrics to command maximum attention. The aesthetic is intentionally rigid, eschewing soft curves for sharp 90-degree angles to project a sense of structural integrity, reliability, and unwavering corporate focus. This is not just a digital interface; it is a high-performance engine for strategic partnerships.

## Colors

The palette is unapologetically dark. The foundation is a pure **True Black (#000000)**, which provides the ultimate contrast for content delivery. 

- **Foundation:** Black is the primary environment. White (#FFFFFF) is used exclusively for high-readability text and structural dividers.
- **Electric Blue (#00F0FF):** The primary action color. Used for primary CTAs, links, and progress indicators. It represents technological sophistication.
- **Neon Green (#CCFF00):** The "Growth" color. Reserved for positive metrics, upward trends, and conversion-focused success states.
- **Hot Pink (#FF007F):** The "Urgency" color. Used sparingly for high-impact alerts, critical data points, or "break-out" design elements that require immediate eye-tracking.

## Typography

This design system utilizes **Archivo Narrow** for all display and labeling roles to provide a sharp, technical, and condensed aesthetic that feels efficient and modern. **Inter** is employed for body copy to ensure maximum legibility against the dark background.

Headlines should utilize tight letter-spacing to create a "blocky," architectural feel. Labels are always set in uppercase with increased letter-spacing to act as "navigational markers" within the dense black layout. Avoid any font weights below 400 to prevent text from "thinning out" on high-resolution screens against the black background.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout philosophy is based on a **Fixed 12-Column Grid** with generous "blackspace." The intention is to create a gallery-like experience where every piece of information feels curated and valuable.

- **Desktop:** 12 columns, 32px gutters, and 80px side margins.
- **Tablet:** 8 columns, 24px gutters, and 40px side margins.
- **Mobile:** 4 columns, 16px gutters, and 24px side margins.

Vertical rhythm is governed by an 8px base unit. Section gaps are intentionally large (160px+) to ensure that distinct marketing phases or service offerings do not bleed into one another, maintaining a clear visual hierarchy of partnerships and growth strategies.

## Elevation & Depth

In a "Clean Square Luxury" system, depth is not achieved through shadows or blurs, but through **Tonal Layering and Border Definition**.

- **Level 0 (Surface):** The base background is #000000.
- **Level 1 (Containers):** Cards and sections are defined by 1px solid White (#FFFFFF) or Grey (#222222) borders.
- **Level 2 (Overlays):** Modals and pop-overs use a solid #111111 background with a high-contrast white border.

Do not use drop shadows. If an element needs to feel elevated, use a "ghost offset" (a 1px white border offset by 4px or 8px) to create a brutalist, architectural sense of layering.

## Shapes

The shape language is strictly **Geometric and Sharp**. There are no rounded corners in this design system. 

Every container, button, input field, and image mask must have a 0px border-radius. This creates a sense of "Uncompromised Precision." If an icon is used, it should ideally feature sharp terminals and square caps to match the surrounding UI components.

## Components

### Buttons
Primary buttons are solid White with Black text, or solid Accent Blue with Black text. They must be perfectly rectangular. Hover states involve a color inversion or a "fill-up" animation.

### Cards
Cards are defined by 1px white borders. They should never have a background color different from the main surface unless they are "active," in which case they may take a dark grey (#111111) fill.

### Input Fields
Inputs are minimal: a 1px white bottom-border only, or a full 1px square box. Use Archivo Narrow for placeholder text to maintain the technical look. Focus states trigger the Primary Accent Blue border.

### Data Visualization
Growth funnels and partnership metrics must use the Neon Green (#CCFF00) for "Up" trends and Hot Pink (#FF007F) for "Attention Required" areas. Use sharp-angled line charts; no curved interpolation for data paths.

### Progress Bars
Strictly linear and square-capped. Use the Electric Blue for the fill and a dark grey (#222222) for the track.
