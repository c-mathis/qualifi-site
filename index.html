Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results? +

Most clients see qualified leads within the first 7-14 days of campaign launch. Significant volume and ROI improvements typically occur within 30-60 days as we optimize campaigns based on performance data.

What makes a lead "qualified"? +

We work with you to define your ideal customer profile (ICP) including firmographics, budget, timeline, and pain points. Every lead is scored against these criteria before delivery.

Do you require long-term contracts? +

No. We offer month-to-month agreements because we're confident in our ability to deliver results. Most clients stay with us long-term because of the ROI, not because they're locked in.

What industries do you work with? +

We have proven expertise across 15+ industries including B2B SaaS, healthcare, legal services, financial services, e-commerce, education, and more. Our strategies are tailored to each industry's unique buying cycles.

How is pricing determined? +

Pricing is based on your monthly lead volume needs and campaign complexity. We offer transparent, flat-rate pricing with no hidden fees. Your investment includes strategy, management, creative, landing pages, and reporting.

Can I integrate with my existing CRM? +

Yes. We integrate with all major CRMs including Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and more. Leads are delivered directly to your CRM with all qualification data included.
